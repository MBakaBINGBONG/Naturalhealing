Sea Moss

Sea Moss also known as Irish moss is a type of seaweed that originated from the Atlantic Ocean and North America. Its name originated from the 1800s when the Irish warmed up milk, sugar, and nutmeg with sea moss during the Irish Potato Famine. Other types of sea moss can be found in the Caribbean, South America, Asia, and Africa. Sea moss is usually made in smoothies gummies, dietary supplement capsules,powders, gels, shampoos, and skin moisturizers

Health Benefits: *Sea moss contains omega-3 which is good for the heart and can lower the risks of cardiovascular disease *Has iodine which is essential to the body since it helps produce thyroid hormones in the body. Thyroid hormones regulate your weight, energy, levels, and metabolism. *Can improve healthy skin and promote hair and nail growth
